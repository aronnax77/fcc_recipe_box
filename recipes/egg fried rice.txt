Egg Fried Rice 01

Categories: Chinese, Rice
Yields: 2 servings
Preparation Time: 00:00

14 ounces pre-cooked long grain rice; refrigerated
4 teaspoons oyster sauce
2 teaspoons soy sauce
2 eggs; beaten
4 tablespoons peanut oil
2 tablespoons carrots; shredded
4 tablespoons frozen peas; thawed
1 spring onion; sliced
2 cloves garlic; minced
salt and pepper

Add half the oil to a hot wok and fry the eggs and stir to break it up.

Add remaining oil to the wok and introduce the garlic and the rice.  Stir
until all is mixed together.

Add the peas carrots and spring onions.  Mix together until all the
flavours have blended.

Add the oyster sauce and the soy sauce and season with salt and pepper.
Toss it all together and remove from the heat.

Serve in individual small bowls.
